To work with Logistics Consultant Kit, use these commands:

**Getting oriented:**
- `/getting-started` — Overview of everything available
- `/quick-start` — Guided questions to route you to the right skill

**Available skills:**
- `/personal-brand` — Positioning, audience map, bio library
- `/client-footprint` — Operational + digital audit dashboard
- `/pitch-master` — Proposal prep, mock pitch, post-pitch analysis
- `/engagement-navigator` — Lead gen, rate negotiation, pipeline, win-loss
- `/supply-chain-ai-impact` — AI disruption assessment + mitigation plan
- `/linkedin-coach` — Profile audit, content strategy, headline

**Orchestrated experience:**
- `/consultant-coach` — Guided sessions with sequencing and context

**Natural language examples:**
```
"Position me for cross-border e-commerce consulting"
"Audit this 3PL before I engage them"
"I'm pitching to a freight forwarder next week"
"Help me negotiate this rate card"
"Will AI disrupt my client's warehouse operation?"
"Review my LinkedIn for supply chain work"
"I just lost a deal - help me understand why"
```
