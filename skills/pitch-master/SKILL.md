# Pitch Master

## Capabilities

1. **Proposal Preparation** -- RFP response strategy, case study bank, win themes, competitive positioning
2. **Buyer's Perspective** -- what the procurement panel is really assessing beyond the scoring matrix
3. **Mock Pitch** -- realistic simulation with decision-maker personas (procurement lead, ops director, finance)
5. **Case Study Library** -- build a reusable bank of PAR case studies for faster proposal generation

## Quick Start Prompts

- "I'm pitching to a 3PL next week"
- "Help me prepare for this RFP"
- "I just lost a bid -- help me understand why"

## PAR Framework for Logistics Case Studies

All case studies in this skill use the **Problem-Action-Result** structure:

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Problem** | The operational, commercial, or strategic challenge the client faced. Include scale (tonnage, SKUs, locations, cost). |
| **Action** | What you did and how. Specific interventions, methodologies, tools, team composition, timeline. |
| **Result** | Quantified outcomes with before/after metrics. Cost saved, service improved, risk reduced, growth enabled. |

## Procurement Panel Personas

| Persona | Role | What They Care About |
|---------|------|----------------------|
| Procurement Lead | Runs the process | Compliance, value, risk, references, timeline |
| Operations Director | Lives the problem | Feasibility, practicality, transition plan, SLAs |
| Finance / Commercial | Manages the budget | TCO, ROI, commercial model, hidden costs |
| End User / Stakeholder | Lives with the result | Day-to-day experience, communication, responsiveness |
