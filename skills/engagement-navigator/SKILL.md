# Engagement Navigator

A structured toolkit for independent logistics consultants to generate leads, win work, negotiate favourable terms, and continuously improve their sales approach. This skill treats every engagement as a data point to sharpen your next move.

## Capabilities

### 1. 90-Day Go-to-Market Plan
**Action:** `Load the Go-to-Market Plan framework.`

Combines elements from lead generation, outreach, and pipeline management into a time-boxed, 90-day strategy for building initial momentum or entering a new market. It provides a week-by-week activity plan.

Use this when you need to:
- Structure your first three months of business development.
- Create a clear action plan to build a sales pipeline from scratch.
- Set realistic targets for outreach, meetings, and proposals.

### 2. Lead Generation Strategy
**Action:** `Load the Lead Generation Strategy.`

Loads `references/lead-gen-strategy.md` — a targeting methodology, tiered account approach, and outreach templates designed for logistics decision-makers.

Use this when you need to:
- Identify the right seniority and role at a target company.
- Prioritise accounts by strategic fit and probability of conversion.
- Craft outreach emails that demonstrate credibility and get replies.

### 3. Rate & Contract Negotiation
**Action:** `Load the Rate Negotiation guide.`

Loads `references/rate-negotiation.md` — hardened rate benchmarks by region (UK/US/EU/APAC), negotiation scripts for common objections, and a quantified concession planning ladder.

Use this when you need to:
- Defend your rate against cheaper alternatives.
- Prepare for a fee discussion with procurement or a budget holder.
- Plan your negotiation concessions without leaving value on the table.

### 4. Pipeline Tracker
**Action:** `Load the Pipeline Tracker framework.`

Loads `references/pipeline-tracker.md` — provides clear pipeline fields, stage definitions, health metrics (including Pipeline Velocity and Win Rate by Source), and a disciplined "next action" logic.

Use this to:
- Track active opportunities with absolute clarity.
- Diagnose why and where deals are stalling.
- Generate a concise summary for your weekly business review.

### 5. Win-Loss Analysis Loop
**Action:** `Load the Win-Loss Analysis template.`

Loads `references/win-loss-analysis.md` — a capture template and a 3-gap analysis framework. The "Pattern Synthesis" section explicitly links findings back to actions in your other skills (e.g., refining your pitch or personal brand).

Use this when you:
- Lose a deal and need an objective debrief process.
- Win a deal and want to codify the reasons for success.
- Need to iterate your sales strategy based on direct market feedback.

## Quick Start Prompts

- *"Help me build a 90-day go-to-market plan."*
- *"Who are the right people to target at this logistics company?"*
- *"A client says they have a cheaper option. How do I respond?"*
- *"Show me how to track my sales pipeline."*
- *"I just lost a deal. Let's run a win-loss analysis."*
